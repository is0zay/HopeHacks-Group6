Our Mission

Our misssion is to bring awareness and change to the different communities that are being affected by oppression. Communities in the United States such as: minorities, low-income, poverty, homeless families, and the lgbtq+ face oppression and discrimination. By joining our organization we will keep all members up to date on issues these groups face. We can help these affected communities get out of oppression and discrimination by support and donations for extended resources.
